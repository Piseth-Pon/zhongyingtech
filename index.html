How Do We Implement

To ensure the effectiveness of studying programming and the expected outcomes which students after finished the class are capable to build projects that are really matter and useful, we need to organize the ICT classroom at least 2 sessions per week, each classroom only 10-15 students (10 is preferred, but if more than 15, a teacher assistant is required). In term of learning programming, it is different from what students learn another subjects, students need to know the fundamental of programming, its concept, and what each line of code does something to the program. We learn by doing, testing, debugging and expanding our program to be more effective and useful. After students master all the basic features, we combine those concepts together by building simple projects to fully functional projects.

Expectation

We strongly believe that students that graduate from our school, not just good at languages and soft skills, but with a very good foundation in STEM (Science, Technology, Engineer, Math). With the enterpreneur skill and technology skill that they learn in school will help them pioneer their career path after high school and also apply them to help their parent business, their study etc.

We are optimistics that our students will become young researchers, innovators who have skill to build projects which can impact in our society.

Scratch Programming

  • Prequisite: Basic Knowledge of Computer
  • Technology: Scratch

Learning Outcome

  • Basic Concept of Programming: Event, Loop, Condition, Variable, List
  • Understanding the concept of Animation and Game Design
  • Designing and drawing their own sprite and backdrop to fullfil their imagination
  • Designing their own project like storytelling, game, animation and art drawing etc.

Connecting Scratch to BBC:Microbit

  • Prequisite: Scratch
  • Technology: Makecode, BBC:Microbits

Learning Outcome

  • Running Scratch with BBC:Microbits
  • Understanding about the features of microbits such as sensor, LED, sound, button, temperator, accelerometor.....
  • Controlling Robot Cars (In Research)
  • Designing their own electronics projects such as smart dustbin, fitness counter, ....

Introduction to AI and ML

  • Prequisite: Scratch
  • Technology: Pictoblox, mBlock, MachinelearningForKids, TeachableMachine

Learning Outcome

  • Understanding the concept of AI and ML
  • Learning to deal with data and train the model
  • Making the program smarter
  • Designing Project on text, sound, image processing, face/object detection etc.

Introduction to Mobile Application

  • Prequisite: Scratch
  • Technology: Google Applab

Learning Outcome

  • Understanding the concept of designing mobile application
  • Understanding the components of mobile application such as button, textbox, screen, dropdown .....etc.
  • Understanding how we manage data and connecting their application to the databases
  • Designing mobile application based on their imagination.

Introduction to Python Programming

  • Prequisite: Typing, Scratch
  • Technology: Python

Learning Outcome

  • Understanding the concept and syntax of python programming language
  • Understanding the algorithm and flowchart, how the program execute

Designing Desktop Application with Python

Learning Outcome
